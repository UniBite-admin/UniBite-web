Account Deletion Request

UniBite operates applications including PopBite. If you would like to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data, please follow the steps below.

How to request account deletion

To delete your account, send an email request to:

admin@unibite.app

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your account email address
  • Confirmation that you want your account permanently deleted

What happens next

Once your request is received:

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • Your personal data will be removed or anonymised in accordance with applicable laws
  • You will receive a confimation email after processing your request.
  • The process may take a reasonable amount of time to complete

Important information

  • This action is irreversible
  • You may lose access to any content, preferences, and history
  • Certain data may be retained where required by law
